Postgraduate Studies


Overview

The Department of Chemistry has been recognized for excellence in both teaching .

The Master of Science in Organic Chemistry is a STEM program designed to provide students with fundamental knowledge in multiple areas of Organics .

Program Duration

This course shall be a full time course. The duration of course will be four semesters spread over two years.

Summary and directions of the curriculum

First Year: Semester I & II

Subject Combinations: Select any one of the following groups of subject

  • • Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • • Chemistry, Botany, Zoology
  • • Physics, Botany, Chemistry
  • • Physics, Chemistry, Zoology

Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology will have two theory papers and two practicals each. Mathematics will have only two theory papers.

Program Overview

The purpose of the Postgraduate program in Information Technology is to facilitate the student's continued professional and scholarly development. The Master of Science (MSc IT) program is designed to extend the student's knowledge and emphasize a particular area of concentration.

Program Details

The M.S.c in Information Technology degree program prepares graduates to work with the latest computer organization technology and computer hardware to gain employment in the ever-growing IT industry or prepare for continuation to a Ph.D. program.

The M.S. degree in Computer Engineering is intended to give those who successfully complete it the following knowledge and skills:

  • •Knowledge and skills required to design and test embedded systems
  • • Understanding multi-threaded programming on bare-metal, custom real-time operating systems, and embedded Linux systems
  • • Implementation of IP stacks for computer networking.
  • • Network and wireless protocol development for Internet of Things devices
  • • Design of real-time operating systems
  • •Design of advanced digital logic systems
  • • Control of systems with electromechanical actuators and sensors

Program Duration

This course shall be a full time course. The duration of course will be four semesters spread over two years.

Previous year at glance

With the collaboration of two IT & CS we have organized “GMV TechFest 2K16”. The festival of three days was full of events. Department organizes this festival every year for development of technical skills among pupils. Wieners of various competition held under festival were facilitated with trophies, medals and certificate. Department organized Industrial Visit to Hyderabad between 24th January & 27th January 2016. Many places were visited in this IV. Most important is visit to Remote Sensing Institute along with Ramoji Film City. Yearly Industrial Visit contributes in overall development of students. All students of third year were participated in Industrial Visit. We have organized two days National level workshop on Android App Development in collaboration with AMALGAM IIT Madras & ARK Technology on 1st and 2nd March 2016. We have organized “Digital India” workshop on 14th August 2016 as per direction of Govt. of India. With collaboration of Swadesh & Fuel Foundation we have organized Career & Personality training program for three days between 5th January & 7th January 2016. The “Secure Digital Life” seminar was organize on 22nd December 2016. For this seminar we got speaker Mr. Abhijeet More (Ethical Hacker).
