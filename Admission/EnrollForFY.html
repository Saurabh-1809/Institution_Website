Enroll for First Year UG Program





Circular :-


The Principle of the affiliated colleges in Arts,Science, Commerce and directors University Sub-campus (Thane and Ratnagiri) are hereby informed that the procedure for admission in Govt/Private/Aided/UnAided/Minority/Autonomuos affiliated colleges and university sub campus of this university is to be followed strictly in accordance with this office circular .


Further , they are informed that considering the Date of declaration of the Result for the H.S.C Examination , the admission process of First year undergraduate/post-graduate F.Y Bsc , F.Y Bsc (Information technology) ,F.Y Bsc(Computer science) etc. courses for the Academic year is as follows as .



Admission Schedule

Sale of admission Forms (Online/Offline) Thursday 9th June, 2022 to Saturday 25th June, 2022(up to 1.00pm)
Preadmission Online Enrolment on university website www.mum.digitaluniversity.ac Thursday 9th June, 2022 to Saturday 25th June, 2022 (up to 1.00 pm)
Online Submission of Admission forms along with Pre-Enrolment form(MANDATORY) fill admission form accurately Thursday 9th June, 2022 to Saturday 25th June, 2022 (up to 1.00 pm)
First Merit List Wednesday, 29th June, 2022 (11.00 am)
Online/Offline Verification of Documents and Online/Offline Payment of Fees with Undertaking Form Thursday, 30th June 2022 to Wednesday 6th July, 2022 (up to 3.00 pm)
Second Merit List Thursday, 7th July 2022 (11.00 am)
Online/Offline Verification of Documents and Online/Offline Payment of Fees with Undertaking Form Friday, 8th July 2022 to Wednesday, 13 July, 2022 (up to 3.00 p.m.)
Third Merit List Thursday, 14th July, 2022 (11.00 a.m.)
Online/Offline Verification of Documents and Online/Offline Payment of Fees with Undertaking Form Thursday, 14th July, 2022 to Saturday 16th July, 2022.



Note :- The college will admit students as per sanctioned intake capacity.

Come with documents to college for physical verification of documents.

After the verification one can secure admission by paying fees online Fee Payment Portal along with Declaration/University form.
