Admission eligibility criteria – Under Graduate - First Year and Under Graduate -





ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA FOR ADMISSION (Science Faculty)

F.Y.B.sc. (Sem.I) HSC + Diploma direct admission to S.Y.B.SC.
HSC or equivalent examination passed.
SSC + 3 years Engg.Diploma direct admission to S.Y.B.Sc.
F.Y.B.sc. (Sem.II) Eligible to be admitted to semester II irrespective of number of heads of failure in the Sem.I examination.
S.Y.B.sc. (Sem.III) HSC + Diploma direct admission to S.Y.B.Sc.
SSC + 3 years Engg.Diploma direct admission to S.Y.B.Sc.
Passed in all courses in Sem.I & Sem.II examination OR
3 ATKT in Sem.I and 3 ATKT in Sem.II
S.Y.B.sc. (Sem.IV) Eligible to be admitted to semester IV irrespective of number of heads of failure in the Sem.II examination.
T.Y.B.sc. (Sem.V) Must Passed Sem.I,Sem.II,Sem.III & Sem.IV OR
Passed Semester I and Sem.II and fails in not more than three courses of Sem.III and Sem.IV each OR
Passed Sem.III and Sem.IV and fails in not more than three courses of Sem.I and Sem.II each.
T.Y.B.sc. (Sem.VI) A learner shall be allowed to keep terms for Semester VI irrespective of grades obtained in each course of semester V AND
The result of Sem.VI shall be kept in absence until the learner passes each of Sem.I,Sem.II,Sem.III,Sem.IV,Sem.V

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA FOR ADMISSION (IT Faculty)

F.Y.B.sc. (Sem.I) SSC + 3 year Engineering Diploma or HSC + Diploma direct admission to S.Y.B.Sc.I.T.
HSC or equivalent examination passed with maths as one of the subject and should have not less than 40% marks in aggregate for reserved category.and 45% marks in aggregate for open category
F.Y.B.sc. (Sem.II) Eligible to be admitted to semester II irrespective of number of heads of failure in the Sem.I examination.
S.Y.B.sc. (Sem.III) HSC + Diploma direct admission to S.Y.B.Sc.
SSC + 3 years Engg.Diploma direct admission to S.Y.B.Sc.
Passed in all courses in Sem.I & Sem.II examination OR
3 ATKT in Sem.I and 3 ATKT in Sem.II
S.Y.B.sc. (Sem.IV) Eligible to be admitted to semester IV irrespective of number of heads of failure in the Sem.II examination.
T.Y.B.sc. (Sem.V) Must Passed Sem.I,Sem.II,Sem.III & Sem.IV OR
Passed Semester I and Sem.II and fails in not more than three courses of Sem.III and Sem.IV each OR
Passed Sem.III and Sem.IV and fails in not more than three courses of Sem.I and Sem.II each.
T.Y.B.sc. (Sem.VI) A learner shall be allowed to keep terms for Semester VI irrespective of grades obtained in each course of semester V AND
The result of Sem.VI shall be kept in absence until the learner passes each of Sem.I,Sem.II,Sem.III,Sem.IV,Sem.V

Important Note :-

.............Students from other colleges affiliated to be University of Mumbai, who are seeking admission to Second and Third year of B.Sc. classes will be admitted provisionally on production of a ‘No Objection Certificate’ from the Principal of the college last attended. Their admissions will be confirmed only on receipt of the Transference Certificate (T.C.) from the previous college. For this purpose, they should submit an application form along with T.C. fees to the college office at the time of admission.
