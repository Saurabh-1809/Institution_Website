Admissions

We’re here to support you, from the application process to your first day of class. It’s time to start your journey!



STUDENTS FIRST. EXCELLENCE ALWAYS.




GUIDLINES AND RULES FOR ADMISSION

  1. Right of admission is reserved by the Principal
  2. A Student seeking admission to the college should present himself/herself during along with parent/guardian with an application in the prescibed form duly filled in and signed by him and his parents/guardian.
  3. No admission will be regarded as granted unless it is duly granted by Principal.
  4. All admissions are will valid only for one academic year and are required to be renewed with application in the prescribed form for the new academic year.
  5. Once student admitted for degree course means he/she must complete the Course in the college.
    However,if under any unavoidable circumstances,student may seek NOC provided he/she must pay the fees of entire degree course.the student and parent should have to produce proper documents such as transfer order etc.for seeking NOC by this college.
  6. Mode of refund of fees after cancellation of admission will be as per circular no UG/412 of 2008 of University of Mumbai.


MORE ON ADMISSION DETAILS

Candidate should belong an Indian Nationality
Candidates who have passed the HSC examination of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education in Science streams are eligible for admission to F.Y.B.Sc. class. All admission are provisional, subject to the approval of the University of Mumbai.

Admission for the First Year class are offered for following the declaration of result of HSC EXAMINATION (Std. XII) by the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education. Admissions are granted as per the statutes and directions issued by the University of Mumbai from time to time.

Student of this college who have qualified for admission to higher class will be admitted to such class only if an application in the prescribed from duly completed, has been received with necessary fees within ONE WEEK from the date of declaration of the result of the qualifying examination.
