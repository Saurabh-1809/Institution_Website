Undergraduate Studies


Overview

The Department of Chemistry ,Zology and Physics has been recognized for excellence in both teaching .

We offer undergraduate degree programs designed to train professional chemists and biochemists through our Bachelor of Science degree in Chemistry, Bachelor of Science degree in Zology, and Bachelor of Science degree in Physics.

Program Duration

This course shall be a full time course. The duration of course will be six semesters spread over three years.

Summary and directions of the curriculum

First Year: Semester I & II

Subject Combinations: Select any one of the following groups of subject

  • • Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • • Chemistry, Botany, Zoology
  • • Physics, Botany, Chemistry
  • • Physics, Chemistry, Zoology

Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology will have two theory papers and two practicals each. Mathematics will have only two theory papers.

Second Year: Semester III & IV

Subject Combinations: Select any one of the following groups of subject out of the subjects offered at F.Y.B.Sc class :

  • • Physics, Mathematics
  • • Physics, Chemistry
  • • Botany, Zoology
  • • Chemistry, Botany
  • • Chemistry, Zoology

Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology will have three theory papers and three practicals each. Mathematics will have only three theory papers, practical in third paper only.

Third Year: Semester V & VI

Chemistry/Botany/Zoology/Physics
/Mathematics/Statistics

Student can opt any one of the subject as a major subject from his/her second year subjects. Each Subject has the four major papers and one applied paper with practical’s in every semester.


Main Subject and Applied Component

  • Chemistry :Pharmaceutical Chemistry and Dyes, Stuff & Paints

  • Zoology : Fishery Biology & Horticulture

  • Physics :Electronic Instrumentation

  • Mathematics : Computer Programming and System Analysis

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology degree program will provide students the knowledge and skills required to develop and apply computer systems to practical, real-world problems.

In addition to required core courses, students can choose from a large selection of electives to focus on such diverse topics as multi-threaded programming on bare-metal, custom real-time operating systems, and embedded Linux systems; implementation of IP stacks for computer networking; network and wireless protocol development for Internet of Things devices; design of real-time operating systems;

They will also apply the knowledge and skills gained in the classroom in real-world settings through internships or co-ops and course projects, including the capstone project.

Program Duration

This course shall be a full time course. The duration of course will be six semesters spread over three years.

Objective

  • •To nurture good infrastructure and innovative teaching learning environment.
  • • To boost students to succeed in industry, government and academia.
  • • To identify real life problems and provide solutions which are economically feasible as well as socially acceptable.
  • • To facilitate the development of academia-industry collaboration programs to meet the changing needs of industry and society.

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in computer science degree program is designed to expose students to the rigorous intellectual and extensive practical aspects of modern software-intensive computing systems, including software development at various levels involving operating systems, database systems, programming languages, networks, security issues, user interfaces, and artificial intelligence.

Students in the Bsc in computer science program will gain fundamental knowledge and practical skills in the design of computer-based systems and in leadership for a changing profession and world.

They will also apply the knowledge and skills gained in the classroom in real-world settings through internships or co-ops and course projects, including the capstone project.

Program Duration

This course shall be a full time course. The duration of course will be six semesters spread over three years.
